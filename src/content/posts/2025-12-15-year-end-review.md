## What Changed

When I started this project in January, I thought 1bit was just an aesthetic choice. By December, I realized it was a **thinking** choice.

Limiting yourself to two colors forces you to solve problems that infinite colors paper over:

- How do you create hierarchy without color?
- How do you create depth without shadows?
- How do you create warmth without temperature?

The answers are always in structure, spacing, and pattern.
